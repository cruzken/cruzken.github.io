About Us

We use only the freshest ingredients to marinate our meat, giving it an unprecedented taste and flavour. These tastes, blended with fresh cut vegetables, sour cream, homemade guacamole, burrito sauce and hot sauce will always leave you wanting more. Burrito Masters receives daily deliveries of fresh meat and produce, meaning our staff will always serve you the best, freshest Mexican burritos.
